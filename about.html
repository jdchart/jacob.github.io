About

Arvest is an open source, free to use tool for the analysis and navigation of collections of digital documents. Consult, compare, organize and annotate documents, images, videos and audio.

Arvest is based on the IIIF standard, and based a modified version of the IIIF viewer Mirador.

Arvest is currently under development in the context of the ERC-funded STAGE Project. You can take a look at the current demo sandbox here. The first beta is set to release at the end of 2024. It is developped at Rennes 2 University, France. Developpment is currently funded by SATT Ouest Valorisation.
